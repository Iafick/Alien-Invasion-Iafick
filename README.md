## MY Gameplay Plan

For my final project, I will redesign the game's orientation by moving the player's ship to the top of the screen instead of the bottom and the alien ships will be at the bottom and facing and going upwards.

## Detailed plan

* The player's ship will be positioned at the **top** of the screen.
* The ship will be rotated to face downward.
* The aliens will begin at the bottom of the screen and move upward toward the player's ship.
* The alien images will be rotated so they face upward toward the ship.
* The Bullets will travel downward toward the aliens.
* Once the bullets touches and shoots the alien it will disaper together with the allien.
* I have maintained the overall objective of the game , but I have just reversed the gameplay vertically to create a different experience. Think of it like the aliens have already arrived on earth and the have settled or concured a certain part of the country. Now we have sent a destroyer ship to shoot down all aliens befor the took off. Hence the aliens being at the bottom symbols them trying to fly and attak other countries and we have to shoot them all down and prevent them from launching another attack.
